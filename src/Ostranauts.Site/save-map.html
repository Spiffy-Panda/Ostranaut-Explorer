Upload an Ostranauts save zip and the map plots every ship at its raw objSS position. Icons are mass-scaled (√fShallowMass) and colored by whether you've ever visited that ship (fLastVisit > 0 vs. 0.0). Ships parked at the same station or moored together share the same vPos (the game stores the body's position in vPos and the tiny ship-vs-body separation in vBOOffset) — so at low zoom they'll stack into a single dot. Hover for a stack list (callsign + mass + visit-state); click to lock the detail panel; zoom in until they spread out. The zoom range is generous — keep scrolling to resolve sub-km separations.
